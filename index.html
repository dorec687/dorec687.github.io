Who i am

About me

A film has the power to convey a story, moods, and feelings. Behind every film, there is an editor who blends everything together to create a story. I like to convey stories and emotions through films. I have been working in post-production for over 10 years and I keep pursuing exellence.

My Background

I did a formal 4 year study in Film/Video at Five Town College, Dix Hills, NY, USA. I got Bachelor's in Fine Art in 2011. I have been working on video editing for over 10 years. I have had various jobs in between my editing gigs such as being a mechanic, and I'm currently being an English Teacher in Taiwan. I'm still living in Taiwan currently but I'm looking into moving back to the USA as soon as I can, hopefully in a few months.

When Not Editing

I like to write and produce songs. Music is a big part of my life. Screenplay writing is another creative form that I enjoy. I also love the automotive world and had a bit of experience as a mechanic. Nowadays I just build plastic model of the cars that I love. I do some karate to stay active. Outside of those activities, you will likely find me watching Netflix/YouTube or just hanging out.
